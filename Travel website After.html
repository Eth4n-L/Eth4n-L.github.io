Travel to South Korea

South Korea is an East Asian country located near the ocean, beside Japan. It's a beautiful country with rich
history and many things to do and this website can help with navigating and exploring the country. Like
many other East Asian countries you should stay in Korea for at least 2 weeks, there's so much stuff to do and you
should make sure you have time to do plenty of thigs.

go to is Lotte world. Lotte world is a massive complex in Seoul that contains an indoor and outdoor
amusment park, shopping malls, museums and many more things. Other things to do in Soeul is to visit
Gyeongbokgung Palace. Gyeongbokgung Palace is a large palace built in 1394 origionally but rebuilt in 1867. This

Gyeongju-Si

Gyeongju is a costal city that is filled with historical buildings and landmarks. Gyeongju has many different
historical sites like Yangdong Folk Village. Yangdong village is a historical village that you can vising in
Gyeongju, the village has many traditional houses and a very natural setting. Another place to visit is
Gyeongji National museum. This museum is great if you want to learn more about Korean history and traditions.
This museum contains many different things like traditional art, pottery and artifacts.

Food

A lot of Korean food can be cheap and affordable if you're on a budget. The food usually has a lot of variety in ingredients
